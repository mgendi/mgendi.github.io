Kids Club

Here at North Harbour SDA Church we seek to be a house of Prayer for all nations - where every Leader, every ministry, every initiative is birthed in prayer, founded in the principles of Scripture and fulfills our God-given calling to Know the Father and Make Him Known. We are committed to helping believers understand and embrace their identity and freedom in Christ and believe our connection to the head of the church - Jesus Christ, is the source of power and eternal significance for both the individual and the body of Christ - the church. Because of this we encourage all a surrendered life and ministry, and a commitment to unity with the rest of the church family.
